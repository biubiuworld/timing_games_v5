Information about Payment

At the end of the last round, the computer will randomly select one of the non-practice rounds for payment.

The threshold is 1000 and the conversion rate is 180 points per dollar today.

  1. If in the selected round you had an average payoff rate of 12, your take home payment would be \( ( 12*180 - 1000 ) /180 = $6.4 \) plus the show-up fee of $6.
  2. If in the selected round you had an average payoff rate of 15, your take home payment would be \( ( 15*180 - 1000 ) /180 = $9.4 \) plus the show-up fee of $6.
  3. If in the selected round you had an average payoff rate of 20, your take home payment would be \( ( 20*180 - 1000 ) /180 = $14.4 \) plus the show-up fee of $6.
